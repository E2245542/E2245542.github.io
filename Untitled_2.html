Fish Cutlet

Ingredients

  • Fish pieces-250g
  • Potatoes-350g
  • Finely chopped onions- 50 to 75g
  • Crushed ginger- 1/2 tablespoon
  • Crushed garlic - 1 tablespoon
  • Green chilli - 4 or 5
  • Chopped curry leaves - 2 springs
  • Grounded black pepper - 1 1/2 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Egg 1
  • Bread crmbs - 100g
  • Salt - as you need
  • Cookong oil - as you need
  • Lime

Instructions

  1. Clean the fish pieces and add water, turmeric powder, and salt.
  2. Cook covered under the high flame until done with all the liquid or the fish fully boiled .
  3. let the fish pieces cool down. then carefully take off the flesh without any bones.
  4. Boil the potatoes, peel off the skin, and crush them with your fingers.
  5. Finely chop the onions, green chillies, and curry leaves. Also, finely crush the garlic and ginger.
  6. Get ready with the ground black pepper, salt, and lime
  7. Take a large bowl and add fish, potatoes, onions, green chillies, curry leaves, ginger, garlic, black pepper, and salt.
  8. Mix everything well until all combined. Finally, add lime juice.
  9. Now make the cutlet in any shape you want.
  10. Take an egg, add some salt and beat it well. Also, take breadcrumbs.
  11. Soak the cutlets one by one in the beaten egg. Then coat it with breadcrumbs.
  12. Heat the oil in deep fry pan and fry the cutlets until it turns to a golden color.
