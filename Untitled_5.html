Aggala

Ingredients

  • 1 cups Roasted Rice Flour
  • 1/4 cup Coconut
  • 100 g Sugar
  • 125 ml Water
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper

Instructions

  1.  Take a pot and add a small amount of sugar.
  2.  Then heat it until brown color.
  3.  Next add the rest of sugar and water keeping stiring.
  4.  Then let to dissolve the sugar.
  5. After it becomes as the texture of honey, remove from the heat.
  6. Then place it aside.
  7.  Take another pot and add coconut.
  8. Then cook them for a few minutes.
  9. Next add the sugar syrup to it. Mix well and remove from the heat.
  10.  Now add roasted rice flour little by little to the coconut mixture and mix well.
  11. If the mixture is dry, you can add a little hot water.
  12.  After that add salt and pepper to it. Now, ready the mixture.
  13.  Then make balls from the mixure and coat them with roasted rice flour.
